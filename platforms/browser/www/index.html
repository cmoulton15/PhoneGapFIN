Fantasy

EPOCH (BY THE GENTS)
Written by Cody Moulton, Jacob Farnsworth, Brandon Terrill

INTRO
Back in a place real far,
There was a town that was famous for
A dragon that could blacken the sky,
As it would steal and kill and plunder,
Making grown men cry.

They lived like this until on one fateful day
There came a knight in shining armor who would pave the way
To freedom from this tyrant who reigned
Because the courage of our knight was unrefrained.

The townspeople came to give support to our knight
And he said to them, with his eyes full of light,
“These dragon attacks, they will never subside.
So I’ll put an end to your oppression, for tonight….
I Ride.”


VERSE I
With the fervor of might, I charge my stallion onward.
Wind bites my flesh, noble locks, they flow unconquered.

I’m here to seize the beast, to take back control of my town.
With my wrath released, I’m gonna give that drake the beatdown.

I’m going down into his cave.
I’ll throw him down into his grave.
I’m gonna come in swinging.


CHORUS
Through the dead of the night, to the thick of the fight.
Whoa!
Storm the dragon’s lair to slay the beast who is dwelling there.
Whoa!
For the love of the girl will fuel this noble quest.
Whoa!
It’s the thrill of the kill, the power of will,
To tan his hide, we will ride.
It’s adventure time!


VERSE II
Passing hills of fire, his smoke consumes my country.
My one and only desire: to fulfill and claim my bounty.

A metal blade straight to his face.
I’ll mount his head on my fireplace.
Let me hear you sing it.


CHORUS


INTERLUDE
This path of cinders hath led me here.
With no surrender, I face my fear.

‘Tis that, what crap? Yon skull with sockets burning…
’Twas I signed up for that? My inner bowels are churning.

For in a bout of brawn, dragon’s breath 'twould roast me.
But, nay!
With epoch verse I shalt claim my victory!


RAP BATTLE
Who dares to wake my slumber?
I’ll cut ya down, and I don’t mean lumber.
Eat ya whole, spit rhymes of gold,
Imma pick your bones, I’m not playing holmes!

Don’t mean to be boasting or bragging
But your rhymes are sagging,
They’re lacking substance, something to keep my attention,
So let me teach you a lesson in the art of rhyme.
You’re just a waste of my time.


So you think you're good at rhyming,
Well you'd better think again.
I'm the king and queen of timing,
I'm the best there's ever been.

This knight in shining armor,
With my long and shiny blade.
I’m here to slay you dragon,
And end your escapades.


What's the matter knight,
Ain’t you up for the fight?
I got you st-ta-ta-ta-tuttering,
But here I come running again.

You bet I'm spitting lightening.
Isn't it so frightening?
Ripping it, flipping it
And I know that you can't handle it.


You might be fast and you might be quick,
But here's a tip for ya: get a new trick!

Your lines are lame and you ain't got game.
This knight’s a boss who will end your fame.


Enough of this drag - Imma roast ya, ghost ya.
Better hold your buns cuz when I'm done they'll be toasted.


Your time has come to take a bow
As I strike you to the ground


Puny human you're no match for me.
I'll smite you, light you, you'll be history


My mighty sword will remove your head
And in one blow, you'll be dead.


Come at me bro I can take your sword.
Snap it in two and you'll be no more.
I'm burning it, I'm winning it, I'm kicking it,
Best be turning and running,
Cuz you're done!

Inspirational

A VOICE (BY THE GENTS)
Written by Cody Moulton, Jacob Farnsworth

VERSE I
Just wanna feel like I’m making a difference,
Standing up for what’s right under all conditions.
But it seems that every time I try I’m picking a side,
I don’t know why. I’m not picking a fight.

But here it goes,
It’s the American way.
Debate, irate, deface, we separate.
Not victory for the people but victory for a side,
Turning us against them, our family divides.

Brother against brother, father against son.
Civil war of words. Where’s the love gone?
Knives aren’t the only weapon that leaves a scar,
Makes a mark, cuts your heart,
I can’t take it.
Why’d the goal change?
They rearrange the plot, just to stay on top.
Well I’m still here.
Screaming from the bottom, my voice suffocated
Cuz this conversation, it doesn’t happen.


CHORUS
And the people all around me scream
"Don't make a sound!"
I want you to hear me.

Inside I know what's right,
I'll speak my mind, let it resound.
I need you to hear me.


VERSE II
I’m searching for purpose,
These words are not worthless.
Digging through verses,
Barely scratching the surface of these issues.
Burning behind my face a crushing weight,
Forcing me to just stay in place.

Culture of gridlock that I’m trying to break.
Opinions won’t budge, but there are lives at stake.
Morality now decided by a collective vote,
Religion seen as hate. Have we lost our way?

Well I’m done staying silent I’m finding the might to fight it
Cuz inside I can’t deny it, I know it’s wrong.
We gotta come together.
I will not stay quiet.
One nation under God is how we’re united.

I’m not casting the blame or pointing the finger,
I got one goal here, to move this crowd.
Are ya hearing my feeling?
Open your hearts now and start the healing.


CHORUS


BRIDGE
Don’t let them divide you, we all have a voice
